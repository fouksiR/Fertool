Live birth rate by embryo transfer and RIF

Melbourne IVF outcomes · January 2019 – June 2022 · Private patients, single embryo transfers

Clinical tool
Data source. Actual live-birth outcomes per single embryo transfer at Melbourne IVF, stratified by age at egg collection, Gardner grade, day of transfer, and cycle type (Fresh / FET / PGT). Imported and cryopreserved eggs excluded; FET cohort excludes embryos with warm survival <75% or multiple cryopreservations. Grade pooling: 4/5AA · 4/5AB/BA · 4/5BB & 3AA · 3BA/AB/BB · Grade 1–2 · any ICM or TE = C.
